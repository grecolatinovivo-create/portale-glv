Dē coniūnctiōne

[M.:] Coniūnctiō quid est?
[D.:] Pars ōrātiōnis adnectēns ōrdinānsque sententiam.

[M.:] Potestās coniūnctiōnum quot speciēs habet?
[D.:] Quīnque.
[M..:] Quās?
[D.:] Cōpulātīvās, disiūnctīvās, explētīvās, causālēs, ratiōnālēs.

[M.:] Dā cōpulātīvās!
[D.:] Et, -que, atque, ac.
[M.:] Dā disiūnctīvās!
[D.:] Aut, -ve, vel, nec, neque.
[M.:] Dā explētīvās!
[D.:] Quidem, equidem, quoque, autem, tamen.
[M.:] Dā causālēs!
[D.:] Sī, etsī, siquidem, quandō, nam, namque, etenim, quamobrem...
[M.:] Dā ratiōnālēs!
[D.:] Itaque, enim, quia, quāpropter, quoniam, ergō, ideō, igitur, scīlicet, proptereā. ...

Dē interiectione
[M.:] Interiectiō quid est?
[D.:] Pars ōrātiōnis significans mentis affectum vōce
inconditā.
[M.:] Significātiō interiectiōnis in quō est?
[D.:] Aut laetitiam significāmus, ut euax! aut dolorem,
ut heu! aut admīrātiōnem, ut papae! aut metum, ut at-
tat! et sī qua sunt similia.
